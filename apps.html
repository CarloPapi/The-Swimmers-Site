Recommended Apps
MySwimPro is a fitness app specifically tailored to swimming fitness. It's meant for swimmers of all levels, and offers personalized swimming workouts and training plans, as well as offers structured dryland programs. The app is available on SmartWatches, giving the app access to immediate and reliable data.
Swim.com is another fitness app tailored to swimming. Like MySwimPro, it is a fitness and analytics app that allows swimmers to monitor their performance in the water. Swim.com is also available on SmartWatches, giving the app access to very reliable data. It can help users set personalized swimming and dryland goals, helping swimmers achieve their long-term fitness and swimming goals.
Fares Ksebati's YouTube channel is a channel dedicated to the sport of swimming. Many of his videos include different bits of technique advise and tips, endurance training programs, and even some warnings against drowning, emphasising even good swimmers are still at risk of drowning. He even did a mock video to demonstrate the effects of drag; he swam a 50 metre freestyle timed sprint in a full-on busienss suit (which included a jacket, a tie, a belt, and all of the other components of a suit) to see how it affected his time.
