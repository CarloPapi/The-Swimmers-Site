Hello! My name is Carlo Papi.

I currently attend Emily Carr Secondary School in the tenth grade, and I have been
in the pool for as long as I can remember. I am currently a part of two swim teams;
Vaughan LifeTime Athletic Swim Team, and the ECSS school swim team. For LifeTime,
I am currently at the Gold level, and I have competed against other gyms and clubs, some
of which were ot her Lifetimes such as Ajax LifeTime Athletic, and Mississauga LifeTime Athletic,
as well as other clubs including the Boulevard Club. The ECSS school swim team has
restarted this year (from 2019) and I joined it. In some of the photos and videos, I am wearing the
ECSS swim team cap, others, the LifeTime Swim cap. I haven't yet competed for ECSS, but a few
competitions are due next month.
